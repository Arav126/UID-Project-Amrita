Tourist Guidelines:
1
All passengers arriving in the state (other than Maharashtra and Kerala) have to undergo self-monitoring of their health for 14 days.
2
In cabs, auto rickshaws and cars, only 2 passengers are allowed apart from the driver.
3
All passengers must install Aarogya Setu app and undergo thermal screening.
4
You must wear a mask and maintain hand and respiratory hygiene.




Some advice for travellers:
1
Try to get vaccinated before travelling
2
Maintain a distance of 6 feet (2 meters) between you and others
3
If you choose to pick up a meal on the road, opt for restaurants that offer drive-thru.
4
Plan to make as few stops as possible, but stop driving if you become drowsy.
